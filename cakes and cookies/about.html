Our story

From a small kitchen to two countries

What started in 2009 as a passion for custom baking has grown into a name trusted by tens of thousands of customers across Lebanon and the UAE. Through it all, one thing hasn't changed: every cake is still made by hand, to order, by people who love what they do.

Our cake artists treat each commission as a one-off — sketching, baking, sculpting and hand-finishing until it's right. The result is cakes that don't just taste wonderful, but stop the room.
