Good to know

Frequently asked

How far in advance should I order?

Custom cakes are made fresh to order, so we recommend reaching out a few days ahead — earlier for weddings, large events and elaborate 3D designs. Message us and we'll confirm what's possible for your date.

How do I pay?

Once we confirm your design and quote on WhatsApp, our team will walk you through the payment and deposit options available in your market (Lebanon or UAE).

Can you recreate a cake I saw on your Instagram?

Absolutely. Send us the reference (a screenshot or link works perfectly) when you order and we'll tailor it to your occasion, size and flavour.

Do you cater for allergies or dietary needs?

Tell us about any allergies or preferences in your order and we'll let you know what we can accommodate.

Can you deliver to a venue or hotel?

Yes — just share the venue details when you order and we'll arrange delivery timing with you.
