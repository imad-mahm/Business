04 MANIFESTO SIX RULES · ONE PAGE
  1. 01

    ONE MENU. ONE SHORT MENU.

    Ten items. We can do ten items very well. We cannot do fifty items at all.

  2. 02

    NO SYRUPS. NO FLAVOURED ANYTHING.

    If the bean is good the cup is sweet. If you'd like something sugary, we keep good chocolate up front.

  3. 03

    EVERYTHING IS NUMBERED.

    Each batch, each bag, each cup. If you ask, we'll tell you exactly when your coffee was roasted, by whom, and on what curve.

  4. 04

    THE ROOM IS LOUD ON PURPOSE.

    The grinder is loud. The music is loud. The people are loud. You're allowed to be loud here. The library is across the street.

  5. 05

    DESIGNERS GET WI-FI. FOR AN HOUR.

    Ask for the password. We will set a timer. The table is for the next person at the top of the hour.

  6. 06

    WE CLOSE BEFORE WE GET TIRED.

    No one wants tired coffee. We'd rather serve fewer people well than more people badly.

— J. ITO, FOUNDER & BAR
