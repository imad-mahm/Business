OPERATOR'S LOG.

Entries 3 of 247
  1. BATCH#001

    2023 / 03 / 14 — Tuesday

    Probat hits the bar.

    Drum arrived in February in a wooden crate from Emmerich. Took four weeks to commission, three more to dial in. First batch on the bar was a Yirgacheffe washed; we sold out by 2 p.m. and closed early to roast more.

  2. BATCH#127

    2024 / 09 / 22 — Sunday

    Imbachí harvest lands.

    First direct lot from Finca El Mirador. Carlos drove the bag down from his hotel. We cupped it twice on the counter, paid in cash, and put it on the bar Wednesday at City+ roast.

  3. BATCH#247

    2026 / 05 / 23 — Today

    Pink Bourbon → cup.

    Day +10 on the Imbachí lot. Drinking exactly where we want it: peak sweetness, balanced acidity, syrupy finish. We'll pull the last bag next Monday before #248 lands.

— T. Kade, head roaster & co-owner
